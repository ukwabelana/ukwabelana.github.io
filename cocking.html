Cooking

Cooking actually started with Baking.

The organization we came with is not the only organization that is sending volunteers to Baphumelele, but we are the only ones who are staying for one year. The others usually come for a couple weeks or months. Luckily a new volunteer arrived a few days after us who is supposed to stay until December. She was the one who came up with the idea to Bake cinnamon roles with the Kids. Pretty sure that she did it with the motivation to eat an “extra snack“, we helped her. First, we had to prepare a recipe and buy all the needed ingredients. After that we had to prepare all the ingredients every time for every cluster before we could start. Together with the Kids we put everything together and mixed it up before they could role the dough into the small roles with cinnamon. Waiting in front of the oven until they were ready was definitely the worst part. Besides that, all of the Kids and even the Sisis had a lot of fun and enjoyed the baking time. After we did it with the first cluster and everybody smelled the incredibly good smell of fresh baked cinnamon roles, all of them couldn`t wait to be next.

Baking went so well that we decided to also try Cooking. This time, we let the Kids choose what they wanted to cook and every cluster decided to do hamburgers. This is something quite easy to make with many Kids because we just needed to prepare the vegetables and the meat, and they could take everything they wanted and put it together. I think for every cluster this is definitely the highlight of their week.

We would like to continue the cooking and baking with the Kids, not only because they enjoyed it so much, but also because otherwise they only get the same few meals again and again. Because Baphumelele can`t afford to buy special food for every time we want to cook something different with the Kids, we need to buy it by ourselves.

If you are interested in supporting this project with even a small donation, so we can afford the ingredients for cooking with the Kids, you are very welcome!! It`s a really easy way to put a big smile on the Kid`s faces!
